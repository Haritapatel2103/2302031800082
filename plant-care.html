Plant Care Tips

"Plants bring beauty, life, and tranquillity to any space, but taking care of them requires the right knowledge and a bit of love. Whether you're just starting your journey as a plants parent or looking to enhance your skills, we've got you covered with these essential plant care tips."

Light Wisdom

Plants thrive with the right light! Bright light suits succulents, indirect light benefits monsteras, and low light works for snake plants. Rotate woolly and use grow lights if needed.

Light it Right

"Not all plants need bright, direct sunlight. Learn to identify your plant prefers bright light, indirect light, or low light."

Watering Wisdom

"Different plants have different watering requirements. Overwatering is one of the most common mistakes learn how to avoid it."
